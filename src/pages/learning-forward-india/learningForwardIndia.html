Learning Forward India (LFIN)

We show you how to plan, implement, and measure high-quality professional learning so you and your team can achieve success with your system, your school, and your students.

Learning Forward

Learning Forward is a non-profit membership organisation focused on promoting research-based practices that lead to productive, sustainable professional learning for educators. Learning Forward hosts conferences, publishes articles, facilitates individual and group learning opportunities, and provides consulting services. Members usually include teachers and school administrators, they are given access to publications, an online community, and get discounted rates for some services, such as bookstore purchases from www.learningforward.org .

 The Learning Forward Academy

an extended and profound learning experience that immerses members in a model of inquiry- and problem-based learning. Academy members work collaboratively to gain knowledge to solve significant student learning problems in their schools or organisations. The Learning Forward Academy seeks to understand individual members' needs and then assist them in meeting objectives through a collaborative learning environment and the support of experienced coaches.

The Standards for Professional Learning outline the characteristics of professional learning that leads to effective teaching practices, supportive leadership, and improved student results. Learning Forward is the only association focused solely on the most critical lever in improving schools - building the knowledge and skills of educators. Through the Standards for Professional Learning, Learning Forward leads the field in understanding what links professional learning to improve student achievement. We assist classroom, school, and system leaders in solving their toughest problems of practice. Learning Forward members experience practical learning opportunities, receive timely publications, and connect with like-minded educators from around the world.
