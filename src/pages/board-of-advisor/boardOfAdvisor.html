William N Bissell Managing Director of Fabindia Overseas Private Limited.

Managing Director of Fabindia Overseas Private Limited. In addition to his unwavering commitment to The Fabindia School, William is best known as a leading social entrepreneur and is the Managing Director of Fabindia Overseas Pvt Ltd, a textile company started by his father, John Bissell, in 1960. In the decade-long journey, Pathways has received many rewards and accolades like ‘Best IT User Award’ from NASSCOM, Learning Spaces design award from the USA and much more. Pathways Schools are a true reflection of world diversity with students and teachers representing over 60 nationalities. Apart from creative and action disciplines, community service is also integrated into the school curriculum at all levels.
